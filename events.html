Events Organised

  1. MicroSim Workshop | LinkedIn Post 1, Post 2 February 2026

    MME, NITK

    Organised a 2-day workshop on Phase Field Modelling and MicroSim at the LHC-C Senate Hall, NITK Surathkal. The sessions were handled by the developers of MicroSim — Prof. Abhik Choudhury (IISc), Prof. Phanikumar Gandham (IIT Madras), and Prof. Gururajan Mogadalai (IIT Bombay) — along with their PhD scholars. The workshop covered dendritic growth, precipitate coarsening, elasticity effects, and hands-on sessions with MicroSim modules, with a strong emphasis on mathematical formulations. Coordinated the event alongside Dr. Sumanth Govindarajan and Prof. Subray Hegde.
