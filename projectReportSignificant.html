Significant Projects

  1. Smart Grid | Cyber Physical Systems | Documentation → Jan. 2026 – Apr. 2026

    Supervisor: Prof. K. V. Gangadharan, Centre For System Design (CSD), NITK

    Built the Energy Generation & Storage layer of a 9-member, 6-node decentralized smart grid prototype using ESP32-based circuits for Node 1 (Solar PV) and Node 2 (Battery BMS). Used ACS712, BH1750, AIT1000, and voltage divider sensors to monitor irradiance, panel temperature, and injected current in real time. Implemented autonomous solar control with thermal derating, relay cutoff above 65°C, night mode below 2,000 lux, SoC bounds of 10–90%, over-temperature protection at 45°C, and automatic charge/discharge switching. Integrated MQTT-triggered closed-loop CPS coordination across generation, load management, and grid protection nodes.

  2. Autonomous Underwater Vehicle | GUJCOST Robofest 5.0 | Ideation Document → Jul. 2025 – Dec. 2025

    Co-designed the mechanical architecture and 3D CAD model of an AUV with modular thruster assemblies, buoyancy control pistons, and a gripper mechanism. Submitted the design for the Ideation Stage of GUJCOST Robofest 5.0, a Government of Gujarat initiative. Performed structural optimization and component integration for hydrodynamic stability, load distribution, compactness, and manufacturability. Drafted subsystem schematics, bill of materials, and mechanical design documentation for proof-of-concept fabrication and future control system integration.

  3. PET Tiles - The Waste to Wealth Saga! | Compression Moulding | Project Report → Apr. 2025 – May 2025

    Supervisor(s): Dr. S.B. Arya & Dr. Selvakumar Murugesan, MME, NITK

    Designed a sustainable process to convert discarded PET bottles into low-cost customizable tiles as a proof-of-concept for decentralized circular waste management. Produced recycled PET tiles using thermoplastic compression moulding after shredding, melting at 300°C, and pouring into preheated stainless steel moulds under compressive load. Added fillers to improve mechanical and aesthetic properties, followed by rapid water quenching to obtain durable functional tiles.

  4. Multiscale Materials Analysis | Project Report → Oct. 2024 – Apr. 2025

    Studied Electron Microscopy (TEM & SEM), X-Ray Diffraction (XRD), energy bands in crystals, and solutions of the Schrödinger Equation for free electrons, bound electrons, tunnelling, and solid states. Used ASE, VESTA, and abTEM to model structures and analyse electronic properties through computational methods. Conducted simulations on GaAs, Re6Se8Cl2, cubic BAs, SrTiO3, Ag, Au, Hg, and conductive polymers such as Polyaniline and PEDOT, computing properties including band structure and density of states.

  5. SAE ADDC 2026 (Runners Up) | LinkedIn Post → Aug. 2025 – Jan. 2026

Contributed to the fabrication of an autonomous drone for the SAEISS Autonomous Drone Development Challenge (ADDC) 2026. Secured second runners-up out of 80 teams for the Best Payload Dropping System category - Certificate.
